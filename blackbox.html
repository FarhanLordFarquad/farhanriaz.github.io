My Black Box Investigation

In my New Media and Tech class me (Farhan Riaz) and my 2 other teammates (Jericho Cubiz and Abdul-Rahman Malik) had to tackle a problem where we had to 3d model a mystery Black Box.the Black box contains multiple different things (tubes containers maybe) and no one has ever seen the inside of it. The only person who has seen it is the maker of the black box (our teacher Mr.Sinusas). The thing that makes the Black Box so intriguing is the fact that when pouring water into it a random amount always comes out. For example when we poured in 1000 ml of water for our first test and 0 came out and then for our next trial we poured in another 1000ml and 370 came out. In summary every time we poured water into the box a random amount always came out.

      • We believe that the black box contains a siphon type mechanism. According to the data me and my teammates collected while making our version of what we thought was inside the black box, the siphon tube mechanism paired with a water splitter at the start and another container where water can go, our design can also achieve a similar random variation of the original Black Box. When we poured in 1000ml (like we did in the original testing for the black box) nothing came out. Then, on our next trial we poured 1000ml into our black box and 975ml came out. Lastly when we poured in 1000ml for our 3rd trial only 760 came out. This evidence supports how my statement says the Black Box could contain a siphon mechanism because the data we gathered when using a siphon mechanism in our design was almost never repeating (aside from the start) and that it can fulfill the requirements the Black Box (original) made. If we were to be wrong and an opposing claim was correct then our data would not have a lot of variation and it would most likely stay very consistent which is something that our team is not aiming for.

      Our 2D Sketch Process

      In our 2D design phase before even making our actual physical model, me and my team were brainstorming different ideas and when we settled with the siphon mechanism we made 2D drawings in adobe photoshop 2025 for a quick sketch of what it should look like. While making the 2D design we also used different brushes and tools to make and represent the different parts of our design. After making the drawing we also made a 3D timeline (animation) of our design and how the water would flow through it and how the different mechanisms (siphon) would work. Next, we would implement our design sketch into a 3d model by using autodesk maya on the macbooks our school supplied our class with.

      Our 3D Modeling Process In Maya

      In the 3D model in maya, me and my team learned all about the different simple mechanics of maya and then when we got deeper into the quarter we started working on more difficult things like booleans, extruding, and multiple other things too. First we made our 3D box and added doors to it using two rectangles that we shaped. Then I also created the different pieces needed in the 3D model and I also added a tube and holes after putting all the pieces in the right place. After that we created a cinematic camera and an animation of where our water would flow and how our model worked we started rendering it into a video. But, before that I added a human hand to my black box on the side and gave it a skeleton so the fingers and wrists could move alone making it more realistic. Finally, after the rigging of the hand was done I made the hand into an animation of it waving. Then, I started rendering the animation (which is still in progress as it is a very slow process with the macs we have.)
